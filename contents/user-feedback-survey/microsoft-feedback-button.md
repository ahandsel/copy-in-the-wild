_Microsoft 365 Apps and Edge all have a Feedback button that provides a quick way for their users to submit a comment or suggestion._ [Microsoft Doc](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/how-do-i-give-feedback-on-microsoft-office-2b102d44-b43f-4dd2-9ff4-23cf144cfb11)


## Where the button lives

![Microsoft Feedback Button icon: white human with white speech bubble, blue background](/microsoft-feedback-icon.png)

Edge's menu: Hamburger button > Help and Feedback > Send Feedback

![Edge's menu: Hamburger button > Help and Feedback > Send Feedback](/microsoft-edge-feedback-menu.png)

Microsoft 365 Apps (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, OneNote):

![Word top bar: right corner > Feedback icon > Feedback Options](/microsoft-365-feedback-menu.png)


## Feedback options and flow

Send feedback to Microsoft:

* 😃 I like something
* 🙁 I don't like something
* 🗳️ I have a suggestion

_When an option is clicked, a form opens with the title of the option. Following is the example for `I like something`:_

![Screenshot of the I like something form](/microsoft-feedback-form.png)

Top bar: `Send feedback to Microsoft`  
H1: `I have a suggestion`  
Checkbox: `Include screenshot`  
_When the checkbox is clicked, a screenshot of the app is taken automatically_  
Checkbox: `You can contact me about this feedback`  
_When the checkbox is clicked, the email field is available for input_  
Final buttons: `Send` or `Cancel`
